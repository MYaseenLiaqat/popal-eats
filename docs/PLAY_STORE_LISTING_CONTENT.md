### Privacy Policy — Popal Eats

**Last updated:** July 1, 2026

Popal Eats (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the Popal Eats mobile application and related services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

#### 1. Information We Collect

**Account information**  
When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, username, password (stored securely), and optional profile photo. If you sign in with Google, we receive basic profile information from Google as permitted by your Google account settings.

**Preferences**  
We collect food preferences you provide, including cuisines, allergies, and dietary choices, to personalize recommendations.

**Location data (optional)**  
With your permission, we may collect device location to suggest nearby restaurants during group dining sessions. You can deny or revoke location access in your device settings at any time.

**Orders and delivery**  
We collect order details, delivery address, payment method selection, and order status to fulfill and support your purchases.

**Social and user-generated content**  
If you use social features, we collect posts, stories, reels, comments, likes, friend connections, group memberships, and media you upload (photos and videos).

**Usage and technical data**  
We collect information about how you use the app, such as pages viewed, features used, and device type, to improve performance and reliability. We use industry-standard services (including Firebase/Google) for authentication and app infrastructure.

#### 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Create and manage your account
- Personalize food recommendations
- Process and deliver orders
- Enable social features (friends, groups, posts, notifications)
- Provide customer support
- Improve app security, performance, and features
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

#### 3. How We Share Information

We may share information with:

- Restaurants and delivery partners to fulfill your orders
- Service providers who help us operate the app (hosting, authentication, analytics infrastructure), under confidentiality obligations
- Law enforcement or regulators when required by law
- Other users, only as you choose through social features (e.g., public posts, group activity)

#### 4. Data Retention

We retain your information while your account is active and as needed to provide services, resolve disputes, and meet legal requirements. You may request account deletion (see Section 6).

#### 5. Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (HTTPS). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

#### 6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access, correct, or delete your personal data
- Withdraw consent (e.g., location)
- Object to or restrict certain processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@popaleats.com. You can update preferences in the Profile section of the app.

#### 7. Children’s Privacy

Popal Eats is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Contact us if you believe a child has provided us data.

#### 8. International Users

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We take steps to protect your data in accordance with this policy.

#### 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version at this URL and update the “Last updated” date.

#### 10. Contact Us

**Popal Eats**  
Email: support@popaleats.com  
Website: https://popaleats.com
