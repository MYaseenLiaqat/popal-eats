Popal Eats is your all-in-one food app — discover dishes, order from local restaurants, track delivery, and decide what to eat with friends.

SMART RECOMMENDATIONS
Get personalized dish picks based on your taste preferences, cuisines, and dietary needs. Browse trending meals, chef specials, and recommendations tailored to you — not a static menu list.

ORDER & DELIVERY
Search restaurants and dishes, build your cart, and place orders with flexible payment options. Follow your order from checkout to delivery in one place.

SOCIAL FOOD EXPERIENCE
Share posts, stories, and reels. Follow friends and restaurants, join groups, and vote together when nobody can agree on dinner. Food is better with company.

COMMUNITY & RECIPES
Explore community recipes, chef content, and food inspiration from people you follow. Find friends, create groups, and plan your next meal together.

FOR RESTAURANTS & HOME CHEFS
Restaurant partners and home chefs can manage menus, orders, content, and analytics from dedicated business tools inside the app.

YOUR PRIVACY, YOUR CONTROL
Set taste preferences and allergies in your profile. Location is optional and used only when you join group sessions for nearby picks. We use your data to improve recommendations and social features — we never sell your personal data.

3. Privacy policy

Privacy Policy — Popal Eats

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Popal Eats ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Popal Eats mobile application and related services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Account information — Name, email, username, password (stored securely), optional profile photo. Google Sign-In provides basic profile data per your Google settings.

Preferences — Cuisines, allergies, dietary choices for personalization.

Location (optional) — Nearby restaurant suggestions in group sessions; revocable in device settings.

Orders and delivery — Order details, address, payment method, order status.

Social and user-generated content — Posts, stories, reels, comments, likes, friends, groups, uploaded media.

Usage and technical data — App usage, device type; Firebase/Google for auth and infrastructure.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Personalize food recommendations
  • Process and deliver orders
  • Enable social features
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve security, performance, and features
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

3. How We Share Information

  • Restaurants and delivery partners (order fulfillment)
  • Service providers (hosting, auth, infrastructure)
  • Law enforcement when required by law
  • Other users, as you choose via social features

4–5. Data Retention & Security

We retain data while your account is active and as needed legally. We use HTTPS and organizational safeguards; no method is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Access, correct, delete, withdraw consent (e.g. location). Contact support@popaleats.com or update Profile in-app.

7. Children's Privacy

Not directed at children under 13. Contact us if you believe a child provided data.

8–10. International Users, Changes, Contact

Data may be processed abroad. Policy updates posted at this URL.
Contact: support@popaleats.com · https://popaleats.com
